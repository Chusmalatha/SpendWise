# Unit Economics & Funnel

## Lead Value & Conversion Funnel
1. **Top of Funnel (Traffic)**: 1,000 visitors from Hacker News / Twitter / LinkedIn.
2. **Activation (Audit Run)**: 400 complete the multi-step audit form (40% conversion).
3. **Capture (Email)**: 60 provide email to receive the full report (15% conversion).
4. **Qualification (High Savings)**: 15 of those leads have >$500/mo in projected savings.
5. **Close (Marketplace Fulfillment)**: 3 startups purchase discounted credits from **Credex** to realize their identified savings (20% close rate on qualified leads).

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
SpendWise AI operates as a free Product-Led Growth (PLG) tool. 
- **Variable Costs**: API costs for Hugging Face Inference and SendGrid (estimated at ~$0.05 per audit).
- **Fixed Costs**: Engineering and design maintenance.
- **Estimated CAC**: Assuming $100/mo in total API/hosting costs and acquiring 12 marketplace customers, the CAC is ~$8.33.

## ARR Math & Profitability Assumptions
If the Credex marketplace takes a 10% commission on the discounted credits sold:
- A startup saving $500/mo is typically spending ~$2,000/mo on AI subscriptions.
- Annual Marketplace GMV per customer: ~$24,000.
- Gross Margin per closed startup: ~$2,400/year.
- **Total Margin from 1,000 visitors**: 3 closes * $2,400 = **$7,200**.

The high LTV/CAC ratio (>$800) makes the SpendWise audit engine an extremely efficient lead generation engine for the core Credex credit marketplace.
